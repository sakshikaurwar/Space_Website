About Haumea

Haumea is a dwarf planet located in the Kuiper Belt, and is one of the fastest rotating large objects in the solar system. It was discovered in 2004 and named after the Hawaiian goddess of fertility and childbirth.

Haumea has an unusual elongated shape due to its rapid rotation, which causes it to flatten at the poles. The dwarf planet also has two moons, Hi'iaka and Namaka, which are thought to have been formed from the debris of a large collision.
