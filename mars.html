About Mars

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is known as the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance caused by iron oxide (rust) on its surface. It's a rocky world with mountains, valleys, and the largest volcano in the solar system, Olympus Mons.

Mars has two small moons and a thin atmosphere, which makes it prone to extreme temperature swings. Scientists are deeply interested in Mars as it may have once supported life, and it is the most explored planet after Earth.
